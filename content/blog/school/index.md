<p>
  
  A place to <b>play</b>, 

  A place to <b>sway</b>, 

  A place to say <b>hurray</b> 
  
</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>
  
  When work is <b>done</b>, 

  A place we <b>run</b>, 

  It is a lot of <b>fun</b> 
  
</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>

  It is the <b>school</b>, 
  
  We like all <b>day</b>, 
  
  Where we study and <b>play</b>

</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>
  
  Because of <b>this</b>, 

  <b>Pandemic</b>, 

  I miss my school <b>Today</b> 
  
</p>
